TOKYO 24H

Your Perfect Tokyo Holiday

08:00 AM

Breakfast at Tsukiji Outer Market

Begin your morning by exploring the vibrant stalls of Tsukiji. Sample fresh seafood delicacies and immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere of this world-famous market.

12:00 PM

Shibuya Sky & The Iconic Crossing

Witness the organised chaos of the world’s busiest pedestrian crossing from above. Shibuya Sky offers a spectacular panoramic view of the city centre that you simply cannot miss.

06:00 PM

The Vibrant Neon Lights of Shinjuku

As the sun sets, head to Shinjuku to experience the glowing neon signs. Explore the narrow, traditional alleys of Omoide Yokocho for an authentic evening ambience and local street food.

10:00 PM

Panoramic Night Views at Roppongi

Conclude your 24-hour journey at Roppongi Hills. Enjoy a stunning view of the illuminated Tokyo Tower and the glittering skyline as the city prepares for rest.
